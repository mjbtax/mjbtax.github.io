Tax Preparation
& E-Filing

MJB Tax has full E-Filing capabilities for its clients. Mail-in filing can take months longer for Tax Authorities to receive and process, all without any confirmation of receiving your file, and tying up any tax refund you may be owed. Don't take unnecessary risks with your tax compliance, ensure your tax preparer has full, instant E-Filing capabilities available for you.
