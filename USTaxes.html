United States Tax Services

If you are a US citizen, US permanent resident, Green Card holder, have US-based income, or annually spend 3+ months in the US?

Than you likely have US tax filing and reporting obligations that you need to have filed annually.

Be aware the IRS will charge penalties and interest when required US tax filing obligations are not met.

Contact MJB Tax and one of our US-certified CPAs will be with you every step of the way to get your US filings in order.

Before we begin our engagement, you will be provided with a full fee assessment on the services being provided.

Our US Tax Services include

  • US Expat Federal Tax Return

    Flat fee includes these forms: 1040, 2555, 1116, Sch.C, one K-1, one rental property, up to 20 bank interest/dividend entries and brokerage transactions.

    If you have more than these, don’t worry. We can easily accommodate your particular circumstance.

  • Additional Federal Tax Returns

    Adding additional years’ returns and becoming fully compliant with US tax laws is one of our areas of expertise.

    We’ve streamlined the process and help get you all the way where you need to be – worry free.

  • State Tax Returns

    Some US states require US expatriates to file taxes on their income as well. It varies by state and the rules are different for each one.

    Your MJB Tax CPA can give you authoritative information regarding your particular circumstance.

  • FBAR Reporting

    FBAR, or the Foreign Bank Account Report (FinCEN Form 114), is required for individuals who have foreign accounts with balances that in total exceeded $10,000 at any time during the tax year.

    Includes up to 10 accounts per reporting year.

  • FATCA Reporting

    FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act, Form 8938), is required for individuals with total assets, in aggregate, beginning at over $50,000. The rules vary depending on your location and/or marital status.

    FATCA filing is a relatively new law that the US government is enforcing vigorously. Includes up to 10 accounts per reporting year.

  • Foreign Corporation

    Foreign corporate returns (form 5471) are required for all US persons who own 10% or more of a corporation overseas.

    Our flat fee assumes your corporate accounting records are in reasonably good order. Your MJB Tax CPA can advise you about your particular foreign corporate return fees before you begin your engagement with MJB Tax.

  • IRS Streamlined Procedure

    The Streamlined Procedure is an IRS amnesty program that allows expats to catch up with their US tax filing and become fully compliant without facing any penalties.

    The procedure requires that expats file 3 years of back taxes and up to 6 years of FBARs (Foreign Bank Account Reports, required when taxpayers own $10,000 in foreign accounts at any time).

  • Relief Procedures for Certain Former Citizens (Renouncing US Citizenship)

    ‘Accidental’ Americans who don’t have a US social security number and who aren’t US tax compliant can face negative treatment from foreign banks.

    This IRS program allows them to renounce their US citizenship without facing back taxes or penalties by filing their last 5 US tax returns.

    Please note they may also have to file additional reporting forms, such as FBARs (required if taxpayers own over $10,000 in foreign accounts).

  • Individual Professional Consultation

    All of our tax preparation clients have the opportunity to consult with their MJB Tax US-certified CPA as part of their tax preparation process.

    However, should you wish to speak with one of our CPAs to address other questions or concerns, we offer a professional consultation by phone or video call.
